Having trouble finding any events happening at the University? Can’t seem to keep up with all the updates from different platforms? Well, you’re in the right place. EventsGow will provide you with all the events happening at the University of Glasgow, all in one place.

Compared to Facebook and Meetup, our website is specifically designed for the University of Glasgow students which can strengthen the internal relations and connection on campus that has been affected dramatically during the lockdown, but also ensure the identification of the initiator. Like the SRC website, which is owned by the University, the functionality will be similar but with EventsGow, students can now create their own social events, start an activity and find more students with common interests.

No need to join various social media groups to stay up to date with exciting events happening near you. Just access the EventsGow website, search up your favourite events and catchup with all the details you need.
